A three-grid Rayleigh quotient method

>Input: ($\mathbf{A}_\ell, \mathbf{A}_{\ell-1}, \mathbf{A}_0, \mathbf{M}_\ell, \mathbf{M}_{\ell-1}, \mathbf{M}_0, \bm\eta_0,\bm\xi_0,\lambda_0, \ell$), where $\bm\eta_0', \bm\xi_0', \lambda_0'$ are the initial left and right eigenvectors at level $0$, and the initial eigenvalue. $\varepsilon\leftarrow10^{-10}$, $M\leftarrow1000$ $(\bm\eta_0,\bm\xi_0,\lambda_0)\leftarrow$ RQI ($\mathbf{A}_{0}, \mathbf{M}_{0}, \bm\eta_0',\bm\xi_0',\lambda_0',\varepsilon,M$ Interpolate the eigenfunctions from $V_0^0$ on $\mathcal{T}_0$ onto $V_0^{\ell-1}$ on $\mathcal{T}_{\ell-1}$: $(\bm\eta_{\ell-1}',\bm\xi_{\ell-1}')\leftarrow(\bm\eta_0,\bm\xi_0)$ $(\bm\eta_{\ell-1},\bm\xi_{\ell-1},\lambda_{\ell-1})\leftarrow$ RQI ($\mathbf{A}_{\ell-1}, \mathbf{M}_{\ell-1}\bm\eta_{\ell-1}',\bm\xi_{\ell-1}',\lambda_{0},\varepsilon,M$) If $\ell-1 = 0$ Output: $\lambda_1-\lambda_0$ else Interpolate the eigenfunctions from $V_0^{\ell-1}$ on $\mathcal{T}_{\ell-1}$ onto $V_0^{\ell}$ on $\mathcal{T}_{\ell}$: $(\bm\eta_{\ell}',\bm\xi_{\ell}')\leftarrow(\bm\eta_{\ell-1},\bm\xi_{\ell-1})$ $(\bm\eta_\ell,\bm\xi_\ell,\lambda_\ell)\leftarrow$ RQI ($\mathbf{A}_\ell, \mathbf{M}_\ell, \bm\eta_\ell',\bm\xi_\ell',\lambda_{\ell-1},\varepsilon,M$) Output: $\lambda_\ell - \lambda_{\ell-1}$
